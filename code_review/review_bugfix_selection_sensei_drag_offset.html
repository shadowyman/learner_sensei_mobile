PR Review Context

This change fixes an initial drag jump observed when moving the Selection Sensei modal. The previous implementation only grounded the modal’s transform conditionally, which could leave a mismatch between the computed rect and the first-move state. We now always clear transform and seed left/top from the current bounding rect at drag start. The offset is calculated immediately after grounding to preserve cursor-to-modal offset. During dragging, we clamp the modal inside the viewport to avoid accidental loss. The fix mirrors the proven logic used by the debug modal drag implementation. The change is minimal, localized to SelectionSensei.handleDragStart and handleDragMove. No interfaces change and no external dependencies are impacted. Risk is low because we avoid transforms entirely once dragging begins. This should produce smooth, no-jump dragging across browsers and zoom levels.
